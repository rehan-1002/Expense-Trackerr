# 💸 AuraWealth — Your Personal Expense Tracker

AuraWealth is a clean, simple, and beautiful way to keep track of your money. It has a modern dark glassmorphic layout that's friendly to use, scales perfectly between your laptop and phone, and works natively in **Indian Rupees (₹)**.

With AuraWealth, you can quickly log your earnings and spending, set a monthly budget, see where you're spending the most, and export your history whenever you need.

---

## ✨ What it does

- **Stunning Dark Mode**: A premium interface built with glassmorphic cards, smooth animations, and soft neon glows.
- **Made for Rupees (₹)**: Formatted natively using the Indian currency system (grouping lakhs and crores) so the numbers make sense to you.
- **Easy Budget Tracking**: Set a spending limit for the month. The progress bar will change colors and alert you if you use up more than 85% of it.
- **Spending Insights**: See a breakdown of the categories you spend on most, so you know exactly where your money goes.
- **Memo Notes & Search**: Type quick descriptions for your logs, and filter or search through them instantly.
- **100% Private**: Your data is saved right in your browser's local storage. There are no sign-ups, no cloud databases, and your financial data never leaves your device.
- **Export to CSV**: Download your entire transaction log with a single click to open in Excel or Google Sheets.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML** for the structure
- **CSS** for the layout, dark theme, and mobile-friendly styling
- **JavaScript** for calculations, local saving, and files exporting
